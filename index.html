An Acre in Nettlewood is a sustainable, off-grid living simulation rpg based on the island of Nettlewood. Leave the concrete, office buildings, hustle and bustle of even the smallest city to nestle yourself into the deep forest where you can reclaim the natural life you’ve always dreamed of. Grow your own food, reclaim materials to build a home, befriend the locals and craft materials to aid your new thriving lifestyle.

Cook

Gather ingredients from the market or forage your environment to make delicious dishes!

Farm

Nurture seeds to maturity and build the garden of your dreams!

Discover

Explore your surroundings to learn about the vast world around you!

Build

Reclaim building components to build the tiny home you’ve always wanted!

Relationships

Nurture lifelong relationships with NPCs! Don’t be afraid to interact with the locals, human and animal alike…
